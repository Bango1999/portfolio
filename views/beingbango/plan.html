25-Year Plan

In 25 years, I see myself working for a tech startup that became popular. There are a few I can think of here in Fayetteville, but one that stands out to me is DataRank. DataRank is a small-time company that has recently seen big interest in the data-mining community. The purpose of the company is to take company data stored over long periods of time and make it into a useful statistic based on whatever the client company requests. Something like this really has a growing niche in the world of e-commerce and commerce in general, because it allows companies to get a firsthand view of what their most successful demographics are, what can be changed to increase product turnaround, order turnaround, really the possibilities are endless.

When I'm 48 years old, I plan on having a nice house somewhere in Indiana, close to where I grew up. If all goes well, I will be married, but if I still feel the way I feel today, I probably won't have any children. My car will be pleasing to the eye and efficient on fuel consumption, a combination that I find to currently be as rare as an albino tiger. I don't require much, but one thing that I believe will be crucial is a fast computer with NAS and fiber internet. I plan on running many types of different applications from this network, all free to access, such as websites, game servers, perhaps even a network for an app that I create between now and then. Who knows what sorts of things I will be into in 25 years.
