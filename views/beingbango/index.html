About Me

My name is Logan B.A.Swango. I completed this website at the University of Arkansas in the summer of 2014. I am studying to complete my Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science, because eventually I want to be a game programmer. Although, I like web designing too, and would be more than happy to do it professionally if it ever came down to it.

I have been a paid web designer since 2011. My experience in the field of website administration is fairly extensive. In high school, I took a course in advanced web design, however we coded exclusively in Dreamweaver and I was taught to format my content using tables. Since then, I have taken more advanced and appropriate courses in web design at NWACC, been hired on to develop the business websites for 3 bowstring manufacturing companies based out of Indiana and Arkansas, received solid PHP and JavaScript foundational principles out of my programming paradigms class, and I have also developed numerous functional and nonfunctional websites for friends, colleagues, and my own pleasure.

I work for CTTP, which is a short abbreviation for the long title Center for Training Transportation Professionals. There, I am a programmer, web developer, and database administrator. We deal extensively with CMS and PHP frameworks such as Symfony, CodeIgniter, and Joomla for creating and routing dynamic content wherever it needs to go.
