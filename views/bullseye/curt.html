Curt has been shooting bows and hunting with a bow since 1991. In that time, he has taken quite a few whitetail deer. His biggest Pope and Young scored 166" gross and netted 160". Curt has shot 3-D archery since 1992 and has shot indoors spots (NFAA) since 2006. He is a very competitive archer who takes his archery very seriously. Curt is an avid fisherman and loves the outdoors. He loves riding his Harley and goes on benefit rides each year in local communities to support fund raisers and charities.
